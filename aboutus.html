About Go Express

Who We Are

Go Express is a transport company dedicated to providing safe, reliable, and affordable travel services across Kenya. We strive to connect people, businesses, and communities through efficient transportation solutions.

Since our establishment, we have focused on delivering quality service, maintaining high safety standards, and ensuring customer satisfaction on every journey.

Our Mission

To provide dependable and affordable transport services while ensuring the comfort and safety of our passengers.

Our Vision

To become Kenya's most trusted and preferred transport service provider through innovation, professionalism, and exceptional customer service.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Reliability
  • Customer Satisfaction
  • Professionalism
